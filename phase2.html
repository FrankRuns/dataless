Phase 2: The Arenas (Simulation Challenges)

Description

In this phase, you’ll tackle a series of simulation challenges designed to test your ability to generate synthetic data and achieve specific business outcomes. Each challenge provides a fixed system (e.g., a pre-built model or spreadsheet) and a goal (e.g., reduce backorders, improve predictions). Your task is to generate the right synthetic data to “win” the challenge.

Simulation Challenges

Choose a challenge below to test your synthetic data skills. Each challenge includes a video, an explanation, and a hands-on exercise in Google Colab.

  • Dashboard Storytelling
  • Inventory Simulation
  • Causal Inference Fire Drill
  • Spreadsheet Logic Hacking
  • KPI Gaming Trap
  • ML Prediction Showdown
