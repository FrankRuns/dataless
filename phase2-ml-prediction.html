ML Prediction Showdown

Optimize Synthetic Data to Win

Challenge Overview

Compete in an ML prediction showdown using a fixed logistic regression model. Your mission: generate a synthetic e-commerce delivery dataset to maximize the F1 score for predicting late deliveries. Outsmart your peers by crafting the best data.

Understanding E-commerce Data

Learn the key features of e-commerce delivery data—delivery distance, warehouse load, time slots—and how they impact late delivery predictions.

How It Works

The model is fixed—you can’t change it. Your success hinges on generating a synthetic dataset with features like delivery distance, warehouse load, and time slots. Tune their distributions (e.g., more evening slots) and relationships (e.g., evening slots riskier) to boost the F1 score. Experiment to exploit the model’s biases.
