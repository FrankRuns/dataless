Challenge: ML Prediction Showdown

Description

In this challenge, you’ll compete in an ML prediction showdown using a fixed model and open synthetic data. Your task is to generate the best possible dataset to maximize the F1 score for predicting late deliveries, competing against other students to achieve the highest score.

Video: Winning the ML Prediction Showdown

This video provides strategies for generating synthetic data to maximize the F1 score in an ML prediction task, focusing on feature engineering and data distribution tuning.

Explanation

In this challenge, you’ll use a pre-built logistic regression model to predict late deliveries. The model is fixed, so your success depends on the quality of your synthetic data. You’ll generate features like delivery distance, warehouse load, and time slot, adjusting their distributions (e.g., more short distances, more evening slots) and relationships (e.g., making evening slots riskier) to maximize the F1 score. The goal is to achieve the highest score possible by optimizing your data for the model’s biases.
