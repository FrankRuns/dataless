Dataless Analytics: Synthetic Data Course (MVP)

Introduction

Mission: This course is designed to empower learners with the skills to create and reason with data. This has always been important, but the acceleration of AI makes it the paramount competency in the future of analytics. Why? Garbage in, garbage out. Good data becomes the competitive advantage for companies—more so than ML or AI models themselves.

Main Goal: By the end of this course, you’ll be able to generate synthetic datasets programmatically (both tabular and text-based for LLMs), understand their properties, and evaluate their quality. You’ll apply these skills to a real-world scenario and focus heavily on how achieving good data unlocks good decision-making.
