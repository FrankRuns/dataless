Dataless Analytics: Synthetic Data Course (MVP)

Introduction

Mission: This course is designed to empower learners with the skills to create and reason with synthetic data, a foundational competency in the future of analytics. As data becomes the competitive advantage for companies—more so than ML or AI models themselves—synthetic data offers a powerful way to innovate while addressing privacy, scarcity, and bias challenges.

Main Goal: By the end of this course, you’ll be able to generate synthetic datasets programmatically, understand their properties, and evaluate their quality using metrics like the F1 score. You’ll apply these skills to a real-world scenario: predicting late deliveries with synthetic data.
