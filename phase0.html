Phase 0: Welcome to the War Room

Description

In this phase, we’ll set the stage for your journey in dataless analytics. You’ll learn to think like a strategist—focusing on high-level goals and outcomes—and act like a builder—using synthetic data to simulate and solve real-world problems. This mindset reset will prepare you for the challenges ahead.

Video: Thinking Like a Strategist, Acting Like a Builder

This video introduces the strategist-builder mindset, explaining how to approach problems with synthetic data as a thinking tool and a practical solution.

Explanation

As a strategist, your job is to identify the key business outcomes—like reducing late deliveries or improving inventory management—and design data-driven solutions to achieve them. As a builder, you’ll create synthetic datasets to simulate these scenarios, allowing you to test hypotheses without real data. In this exercise, you’ll generate a simple synthetic dataset to practice this mindset, setting the foundation for the course.
