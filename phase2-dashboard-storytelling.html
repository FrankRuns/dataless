Challenge: Dashboard Storytelling

Description

In this challenge, your goal is to shape a compelling story using minimal, high-impact synthetic data for a dashboard. You’ll generate a dataset that highlights key business insights, such as sales trends or customer behavior, with a focus on clarity and impact.

Video: Crafting Stories with Synthetic Data

This video explains how to design synthetic data for dashboard storytelling, focusing on selecting the right features and distributions to highlight critical insights.

Explanation

Effective dashboard storytelling requires synthetic data that tells a clear story with minimal noise. For example, to highlight sales trends, you might generate data with features like sales volume, region, and time, ensuring the data shows a dramatic increase in sales in one region. In this exercise, you’ll generate a synthetic dataset for a sales dashboard, focusing on creating high-impact insights with minimal data points.
