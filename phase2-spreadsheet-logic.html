Challenge: Spreadsheet Logic Hacking

Description

In this challenge, you’ll break and then repair a classic business model using synthetic data in a spreadsheet. You’ll generate data to simulate a business scenario, identify flaws in the model, and propose fixes to improve its performance.

Video: Hacking Business Models with Synthetic Data

This video shows how to use synthetic data to stress-test a business model in a spreadsheet, identify weaknesses, and implement improvements.

Explanation

Business models in spreadsheets often rely on assumptions that don’t hold up under real-world conditions. In this challenge, you’ll generate synthetic data for a business model (e.g., a retail sales forecast) with features like sales volume, pricing, and costs. You’ll first “break” the model by introducing edge cases (e.g., extreme sales spikes), then repair it by adjusting the logic (e.g., adding buffers for variability). The goal is to create a robust model that performs well under diverse scenarios.
